Course Structure

Phase 1: Radio Fundamentals (Lessons 1–8)

Ground-up introduction to radio physics, frequency, modulation, power, channels, and frame structure.

Phase 2: Management Frames (Lessons 9–18)

Beacon, Probe, Auth, Association, Reassociation, Deauth. State machines. Attack detection.

Phase 3: 802.11ac (WiFi 5) (Lessons 19–27)

VHT PPDU, capabilities, MCS, NSS, STBC, LDPC, beamforming, 80/160 MHz channels.

Phase 4: 802.11ax (WiFi 6) (Lessons 28–36)

HE PPDU, OFDMA, resource units, MU-EDCA, TWT, multi-user uplink, BSS color.

Phase 5: 802.11be & Security (Lessons 37–45)

802.11be overview, WPA3 SAE, 192-bit encryption, MFP, attack detection, compliance validation.

Phase 6: Core MAC & Operations (Lessons 46–52)

CSMA/CA & DCF, QoS/EDCA, aggregation & Block Ack, power management, 802.11k/v roaming, DFS/regulatory, 802.1X/EAP.
